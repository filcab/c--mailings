Meeting objectives

The primary goals of this meeting will be:

  • Work on C++20 features

Additional, lower-priority goals include:

  • Merge the foundational concepts (only) from the Ranges TS into the C++20 working draft, continuing to refine it in the IS draft and adopting its issues list into the main issues lists
  • Merge the Coroutines TS into the C++20 working draft, continuing to refine it in the IS draft and adopting its issues list into the main issues lists
