2.2 Notable editorial changes:

  • e15540b1 major reorg of <iterator>; add concepts to the synopsis

    Cleanup of the <experimental/ranges/iterator> header, most significantly including the iterator concepts in the header synposis. (Partially addresses issue 11.)

  • 1862bd89 add <experimental/ranges/concepts> synopsis

    Adds the missing synopsis of the <experimental/ranges/concepts> header. (Partially addresses issue 11; directly addresses NB comment US-6 / issue 349.)

  • aced8dc9 Relocate deprecated algorithm overloads completely to Annex A

    As requested by NB US-8 / issue 359, move the deprecated overloads of equal, is_permutation, mismatch, swap_ranges, and transform completely into the Annex.

  • a8f86656 Replace distance(f, l) with l - f in [alg.is_permutation]/3

    As requested by NB JP-2 / issue 356.

  • ade5d7a0 Relocate header table; macros are not entities

    As requested by NB US-4 / issue 347, clarify that macros are not entities.

  • 85b59183 “equal” for customization point objects means [concepts.lib.general.equality]

    As requested by NB US-3 / issue 346, clarify the meaning of “equal” as used in the definition of customization point object.

  • 99da7818 Directory? What’s a directory?

    As requested by NB US-1 / issue 344, don’t use the term “directory” to describe the new headers.

  • 3bcd694f Setup clauses 1-3 per ISO/IEC directives part 2

    ISO/IEC directives part 2 demands that clauses 1-3 are exactly “Scope,” “References,” and “Terms and Definitions.” This pushes the pre-existing section numbers up by three relative to their numbering in N4620. This change was also requested by NB CA-1 / issue 341.

  • 6d2ff48e argument expressions to ranges::swap reference, rather than denote, objects

    Clarification confusion caused by the specifications of ranges::swap and ranges::iter_swap both using the term “denote” but with differing meanings, per issue 306.

  • 283fa47b Clarify iterators.bidi/3.3

    Clarify [iterators.bidirectional]/3.3 “If bool(a == b), then bool((a--, a) == --b).”, as requested during LWG review in Issaquah recorded in issue 292.

  • 9f81821b Reorganize iterator.requirements.general to clarify presentation

    As requested by NB GB-3 / issue 352.

  • 484b24a2 Define “projection”; Fixes #221.

    As requested by LWG during the third Ranges review telecon, recorded in issue 221.

  • f191094b Patch for #353 (#376)

    Clarify the &i == &++i semantic requirement for WeaklyIncrementable, as requested by NB GB-4 / issue 353.
