Meeting objectives

The primary goal of this meeting will be:

  • Complete resolution of ballot comments (N4644) for Ranges PDTS (PWD N4651), and advance to publication
  • Complete resolution of ballot comments (N4643) for Networking PDTS (PWD N4656), and advance to publication
  • Resolve ballot comments for Coroutines PDTS (N4663)
  • Advance Modules (PWD N4647) to PDTS

Additional, lower-priority goals include:

  • Try to advance Parallelism v2 (WD N4578) to PDTS
  • Try to advance Concurrency v2 to NP
